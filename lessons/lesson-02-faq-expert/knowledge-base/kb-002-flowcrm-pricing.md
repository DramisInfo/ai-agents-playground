# KB-002: FlowCRM Pricing and Plans

**Product:** FlowCRM  
**Category:** Pricing & Licensing  
**Last Updated:** December 2025  
**Article ID:** KB-002

## Summary
FlowCRM offers three pricing tiers to accommodate businesses of different sizes and needs. All plans include core CRM functionality with varying limits and features.

## Pricing Tiers

### Starter Plan - $49/user/month

**Best for:** Small teams getting started with CRM

**Included:**
- Up to 10 users maximum
- 10,000 contact limit
- Core contact and deal management
- Basic reporting dashboards
- Email support (24-hour response time)
- Mobile apps (iOS & Android)
- Standard integrations

**Not Included:**
- Marketing automation
- API access
- Advanced reporting
- Priority support

---

### Professional Plan - $99/user/month

**Best for:** Growing businesses with marketing needs

**Everything in Starter, plus:**
- Unlimited users
- 100,000 contact limit
- Marketing automation (5,000 emails/month)
- Advanced reporting and forecasting
- Custom dashboards
- API access (100 calls/minute)
- Priority email and chat support
- Workflow automation
- Advanced security features
- Custom fields and objects

---

### Enterprise Plan - $199/user/month

**Best for:** Large organizations with complex requirements

**Everything in Professional, plus:**
- Unlimited contacts
- Marketing automation (50,000 emails/month)
- Custom integrations and webhooks
- Unlimited API calls
- Dedicated account manager
- 24/7 phone support
- Advanced security (SSO, IP restrictions)
- Custom training and onboarding
- Service level agreement (SLA)
- Priority feature requests
- Sandbox environment
- Data residency options (US, EU, Asia-Pacific)

## Annual Billing Discount

Save 20% by paying annually:
- Starter: $470/user/year (save $118)
- Professional: $950/user/year (save $238)
- Enterprise: $1,910/user/year (save $478)

## Add-ons (All Plans)

- **Extra Storage:** $10/month per 100GB
- **Additional Email Credits:** $5/month per 1,000 emails (Professional & Enterprise)
- **Phone Support:** $50/user/month (Starter & Professional)
- **Premium Integrations:** Varies by integration

## Free Trial

All plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to Enterprise features. No credit card required to start. Trial automatically converts to Starter plan at the end unless upgraded.

## Non-Profit Discount

Registered non-profit organizations receive 30% off all plans. Contact sales@techflowsolutions.com with your tax-exempt documentation.

## Payment Methods

- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- ACH bank transfer (annual plans only)
- Purchase order (Enterprise plans only)

## Refund Policy

30-day money-back guarantee on annual plans. Monthly plans can be canceled anytime - no refunds for partial months.
