# KB-001: FlowCRM Product Overview

**Product:** FlowCRM  
**Category:** Product Information  
**Last Updated:** December 2025  
**Article ID:** KB-001

## Summary
FlowCRM is TechFlow Solutions' flagship customer relationship management platform designed for mid-sized businesses. It combines contact management, sales pipeline tracking, and marketing automation in a single, intuitive interface.

## Product Details

**Release Date:** January 2023  
**Current Version:** 2.5.1  
**Deployment:** Cloud-hosted (AWS)  
**Mobile Support:** iOS and Android native apps  
**Security:** SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant  
**Uptime SLA:** 99.9% guaranteed

## Key Capabilities

### Contact Management
- Unlimited contact storage with custom fields
- Company hierarchies and relationship tracking
- Full interaction timeline and history
- Smart contact enrichment from public sources
- Automatic duplicate detection and merging
- Import from CSV, Excel, or popular CRM systems

### Sales Pipeline
- Customizable sales stages and deal tracking
- Revenue forecasting and reporting
- Email integration (Gmail, Outlook, Office 365)
- Task and activity tracking
- Deal probability scoring
- Comprehensive win/loss analysis

### Marketing Automation
- Email campaign builder with professional templates
- A/B testing capabilities
- Lead scoring and nurturing workflows
- Landing page builder with drag-and-drop
- Custom form builder
- Marketing attribution reporting

## Integrations

FlowCRM integrates with over 50 business applications:

- **Communication:** Slack, Microsoft Teams, Zoom
- **Email:** Gmail, Outlook, Office 365
- **Calendar:** Google Calendar, Outlook Calendar
- **Storage:** Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics, Mixpanel
- **Support:** Zendesk, Intercom, Freshdesk

## System Requirements

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge - latest 2 versions)
- Minimum 4GB RAM recommended for optimal performance
- Stable internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps recommended)
- JavaScript enabled

## API Access

FlowCRM provides a RESTful API with OAuth 2.0 authentication. API access is available on Professional and Enterprise plans. Full API documentation available at: developers.techflowsolutions.com/flowcrm
